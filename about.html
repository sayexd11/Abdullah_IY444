🎼 What is a Piano?

The piano is one of the most beloved musical instruments in the world. Invented around the year 1700 by Bartolomeo Cristofori in Italy, it has become essential in classical music, pop, jazz, and modern genres.

🎹 Grand Piano

The grand piano is large, elegant, and used in concerts. It offers a deep and rich sound.

🎼 Upright Piano

This piano is more compact and fits well in homes and classrooms.

💻 Digital Piano

Great for beginners, it uses modern technology to simulate the piano sound and is very portable.

🕰️ A Brief History

From the harpsichord to the modern digital keyboard, the piano has evolved over 300 years to become a versatile and accessible instrument for all.
